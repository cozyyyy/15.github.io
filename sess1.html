1st DDR / ITG session (Jan. 26, 2019)

The first IAP test session of the new StepManiaX cabinet was held on Saturday, Jan. 26, from 6pm to 12am. About 15 people in total showed up to the session, which was very good considering that it was announced two days in advance and was only advertised to the StepMania CSAIL mailing list.
